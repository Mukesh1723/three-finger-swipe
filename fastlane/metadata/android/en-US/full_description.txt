Three Finger Swipe adds a three-finger swipe down gesture to take screenshots on AOSP and Pixel devices.

This is an LSPosed module that hooks into system_server to register a pointer event listener. Simply swipe down with three fingers to capture a screenshot.

Features:
- Three-finger swipe down to screenshot
- Companion app with module status and toggle
- Edge exclusion to avoid conflicts with system gestures
- Orientation-aware swipe threshold
- Debug logging for troubleshooting

Requirements:
- Android 9.0+ (API 28)
- LSPosed framework (JingMatrix fork recommended)
- Root access
